Keyboard shortcuts

⌘+K
Focus the search input
↑ ↓
Navigate through search results
Enter
Open the highlighted search result
Esc
Close search results or this dialog
?
Open this dialog
Click # next to a heading
Copy the heading's permalink to your clipboard

Press Esc or click outside this panel to close.
